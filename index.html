● Online now
My dad passed last month. I still reach for my phone to call him.
That instinct — reaching for the phone — shows how alive the relationship still is in you. You don't have to stop missing him.

What was something he always said to you?
He always said "just keep going, kid." Even when everything was hard.
He's still saying it. Through you, right now, choosing to keep going. That's not gone. 🕯️
I have a huge presentation tomorrow and I can't stop spiralling.
First — breathe. In for 4 counts, hold for 4, out for 6. Right now, with me.

Your mind is trying to protect you. It's just being too loud. What's the specific fear underneath the spiral?
That I'll freeze and look stupid. That I'll lose everything.
That thought is a story, not a prophecy. You've prepared. People remember how you made them feel — not whether you were perfect.

What's one thing you know really well about this topic?
I'm 28 and feel like everyone has it figured out except me.
Most people who look like they have it figured out are performing certainty. The ones thriving at 38 asked the hard questions at 28.

What did you care about before you started comparing?
I used to paint. Haven't touched it in 3 years.
There's your thread. Not the career — just that. Why did you stop? ✨
I feel like God is distant. My prayers feel empty.
What you're feeling has a name — spiritual dryness. Even the greatest saints wrote about it. St. John of the Cross called it "the dark night of the soul."

The feeling of distance is not evidence of absence. What were you praying for?
Healing for my sister. It's been months.
Keep praying. Romans 8:26 — the Spirit intercedes "with groans too deep for words." You don't need perfect prayers. The love behind them is enough. 🙏
I've been feeling really guilty. I don't know if Allah can forgive me.
Assalamu alaikum. The fact that you feel this guilt speaks to the goodness of your heart.

The Quran is clear: "Do not despair of the mercy of Allah. Indeed, Allah forgives all sins." (39:53)

What would it mean to you to feel forgiven?
It would mean I could start fresh. Stop carrying this.
That fresh start is available to you. Not eventually — now. What is one small act of goodness you could offer today? 🌿
